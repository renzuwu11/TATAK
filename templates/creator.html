 Meet the man behind TATAK! What's the inspiration behind TATAK?
     "The inspiration behind TATAK stemmed from a personal experience.
When my mom asked me to design a logo for our business, it sparked a
realization. I thought, 'What if other elderly or non-art savvy individuals
wanted to create logos for themselves?' That's when the idea for TATAK
was born. It wasn't just about satisfying my own creative itch; it was
about making logo design accessible to everyone!" 
 What drives your passion for empowering others through TATAK?
     "What drives my passion for empowering others through TATAK is the
belief that creativity should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their
background or expertise. Growing up, I witnessed the power of design to
communicate ideas and express identity, and I've always been passionate
about democratizing this process. TATAK allows me to fulfill that passion
by providing a platform where individuals, especially those who may not
have traditional design skills, can easily create logos and visually represent
their ideas.
